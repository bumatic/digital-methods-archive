Parliament speech data is retrieved via PENELOPE. It covers speeches from the Bundestag (the German national parliament) starting from Period 19, as well as speeches from the British House of Commons starting from January 2016. Both datasets end in October 2019. The data has been collected by the MIS Institute at MPG Leipzig.

Results are formatted as one 'thread' per distinct debate, with each individual contribution to that debate as a 'post' in the thread.
